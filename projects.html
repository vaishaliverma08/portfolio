Let's Bid

Description

We are developing a unique bidding website that sets itself apart from others. Our concept revolves around determining a winner based on the combination of the lowest and most unique bid. To participate, users are required to purchase a coin. By placing bids equal to the value of the coin, users have the opportunity to win products at the price of that coin.

Tech Stack:

PHP, HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, MYSQL

Yield Yatra

Description

Our project is an application aimed at improving the lives of farmers. The app provides farmers with the opportunity to utilize cold storage facilities to store their produce, ensuring its freshness and quality. Additionally, the app eliminates the need for middlemen who often take a significant portion of the profits. By connecting farmers directly with customers, they can sell their yields at fair prices, maximizing their profits and improving their livelihoods. Overall, our application empowers farmers by providing them with essential tools and access to a wider market, ultimately benefiting both the farmers and the customers.

Tech Stack:

XML,Android Studio,Java
